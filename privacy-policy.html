🛡️ Privacy Policy
Aakhri baar update kiya: 28 May 2025 • Grahika Astro
📋 Parichay

Grahika Astro aapki privacy ki suraksha ko bahut gambhirta se leta hai. Yeh Privacy Policy batati hai ki hum aapki jaankari kaise ikattha karte hain, use karte hain, aur surakshit rakhte hain.

Hamari service use karke aap is policy se sahmat hote hain.

📦 Kaunsi Jaankari Ikattha Karte Hain
  • Naam, email address aur password (account banate waqt)
  • Janm tithi, janm samay aur janm sthan (Kundali ke liye)
  • Google ya GitHub account ki basic jaankari (social login par)
  • Payment jaankari (Cashfree ke zariye — card details store nahi hoti)
  • App usage data aur chat history (AI Guru feature ke liye)
🎯 Jaankari Ka Upyog
  • Kundali aur Rashifal generate karne ke liye
  • AI Astro Guru se jawab dene ke liye
  • Account manage karne aur login ke liye
  • Payment process karne ke liye
  • Customer support dene ke liye
🔒 Jaankari Ki Suraksha
  • Firebase Authentication se secure login
  • Cashfree ke zariye 256-bit SSL encrypted payments
  • Password kabhi plain text mein store nahi hota
  • Aapki jaankari kisi third party ko nahi bechte
🤝 Third Party Services
  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication aur database
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — AI Astro Guru
  • Cashfree — Secure payment processing
👶 Bachchon Ki Privacy

Hamari service 13 saal se kam umra ke bachchon ke liye nahi hai. Hum jaante-bujhte 13 saal se kam umra ke bachchon ki jaankari ikattha nahi karte.

🗑️ Aapke Adhikar
  • Apni jaankari dekhne ka adhikar
  • Apni jaankari theek karwane ka adhikar
  • Apna account delete karwane ka adhikar
